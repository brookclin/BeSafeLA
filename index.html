Number of Crime Cases by Year

We break down the crime data from 2010 to 2016 into the following aspects to look into the crime trend in different ways.
Select from the menu to explore.

Timeline Victim's Sex Victim's Age Victim's Ethnicity Top 5 Crime Types
  • Area chart demonstrates the trend of the number of crimes during 2010 to 2016 period.
  • Move and brush with mouse to find refined trend of the number of crimes in specific months of specific years.
  • Hovering over a bar representing specific year in histogram will find the distribution for the number of victims based on sex in this year showed in pie chart and legend.
  • Hovering over pie slice of specific sex of victim will find the distribution for the number of crimes based on this type of sex during 2010 to 2016 period in the histogram.
  • Hovering over a bar representing specific year in histogram will find the distribution for the number of victims based on age section in this year showed in pie chart and legend.
  • Hovering over pie slice of specific age group of victim will find the distribution for the number of crimes based on this age group during 2010 to 2016 period in the histogram.
  • Hovering over a bar representing specific year in histogram will find the distribution for the number of victims based on ethnicity in this year showed in pie chart and legend.
  • Hovering over pie slice of specific ethnicity of victim will find the distribution for the number of crimes based on this type of ethnicity during 2010 to 2016 period in the histogram.
  • The Grouped Horizontal Bar Chart combined with Dendrogram shows the top 5 crime types during 2010 to 2016 period.
  • Hovering over a bar representing a specific crime type will find its completed name as well as quantity showed in rectangle and ball.
